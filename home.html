About

Mission Statement

My transition to web development was precipitated by my desire to further expand my knowledge of programming and to foster more visual creativity in my work as I am interested in creating intuitive web experiences for any level of use. My apps allow the user to fully appreciate the power and beauty of our connected world.

Through my personal methodology, I provide solutions to complex web issues, ranging from simplifying and beautifying client-side experience, to better organization and efficiency of back-end applications. My past experience with leading group projects, and my ability to simultaneously recognize the gestalt of a situation while paying minute attention to detail aids me in the face of complex problems.

My knowledge of Python and SQL for data analysis, HTML, CSS, JS, JQuery, and Bootstrap for front-end web development, and my background in applied mathematics combine to form a strong positive contribution to the success of my employer. Through my desire to continually deepen and broaden my professional skill set, I contribute a forward-thinking mindset and motivation to my coworkers.
